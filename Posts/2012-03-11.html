Return to Flaggy Rock

It seemed like an excellent idea at the time; go to our block at Flaggy Rock in central Queensland and plant 3000 Spotted Gum trees. It shouldn't take more than a few weeks, six weeks max. Admittedly it has been a while since we had been to visit the block. With two of the biggest wet seasons in decades back to back, nature had decided to reclaim our block...

We arrived at front gate of the block on Sunday 11th March 2012, full of dreams and enthusiasm...
